Purchase terms

Marple accecpts sales of cash, hirepurcahse, deferred payment and air or mobile transactions

Cash terms

Marple accepts cash from clients or customers and a valid receipt with marple stamp is granted unto the buyer for records. Only Ugandan shillings are accepted.

Hire purchase

Make small bits of installment at marple shop and receive your item at the last installment and installments are broken down according to periods.

Deferred payment.

Receive your item on the first 40% down payment and complete the rest in 5 installments within half a year. In case of default, Marple has the right to regain back sold item and no refund made or we sue you to court.

Delivery:

Free delivery is made not across two districts. Where delivery point is beyond two districts, the owner pays a small token of transport to the shop if Marple is to do delivery.. Contact us on the contact page for more info .
