KNOW ABOUT US

Marple hand furniture shop was established in the year 2003 23rd February by a senior CEO by the name of Ajiga Richard a senior wood worker. Marple hand furniture shop deals with only hard and soft wood not metals and produces the best designs across the world.

Marple stands with two branches at Kabuusu and Nalukolongo kampala uganda. They coordinate together to achieve wood and customer problems.

Marple also partners with luxury furniture shop kampala to maintain high quality designs by corporation and coordination.

We have very qualified staff with diploma and degree qualifications experienced over years in wood work and carpentry.
